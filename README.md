# **Super Store Analytics & Sales Forecasting Dashboard**

## **Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing Super Store sales data using Power BI to gain insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends. The report also includes sales forecasting to support data-driven decision-making.

## **Dataset Information**

The dataset used in this project is a Super Store sales dataset containing information about orders, customers, products, regions, sales, profit, and quantity.

## **Tools & Technologies Used**

- Power BI
- Power Query Editor (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- DAX (Calculated Measures & KPIs)
- Microsoft Excel (Data Source)

## **Key Insights**

- Copiers generate high profit despite lower sales quantity, indicating strong margins and premium pricing.

- Machines show high sales value but inconsistent or negative profit in some cases, highlighting cost or discounting issues.

- The West region contributes the highest profit, while some regions show high sales but lower profitability.

- Office Supplies have stable demand but comparatively lower profit margins than Technology products.

- Sales increase during year-end months, showing clear seasonal trends.

- Month-on-Month analysis reveals fluctuating sales patterns, emphasizing the need for demand forecasting.

- Customer segmentation shows that a small group of customers contributes a significant portion of total revenue.
